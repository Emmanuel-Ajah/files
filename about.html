biography

Hi, I'm Emmanuel

I'm a software developer, app designer, and technology entrepreneur with a passion for building digital products that solve real-world problems. My work spans mobile application development, UI/UX design, and modern web technologies — creating intuitive, user-friendly applications for individuals, startups, and businesses.

I am the Founder of AppNaija Studios, a software development studio focused on designing and developing high-quality mobile and web applications, and the Founder of Tutgo Technologies, an educational technology platform dedicated to equipping aspiring developers with practical programming skills through accessible, project-based learning.

Driven by innovation and continuous learning, I am committed to using technology to empower people, improve digital experiences, and contribute to the growth of Africa's technology ecosystem.
